BOB WALKER — DIARY ENTRY 02

EVALUATION TRANSFER

I was called up for evaluation today. They said it was part of the cure research program — a deeper assessment of how exposed individuals respond under controlled conditions.

Everything becomes more controlled. Conversations are short. No one explains things unless you ask directly, and even then the answers feel carefully chosen.

They asked questions about obedience under stress, reaction to isolation, and how quickly I follow medical instructions without full explanation.

One of them said I was “selected for continued assessment under recovery trial protocols.” It still sounds like treatment… but I’m not sure where the treatment is leading anymore.

- Bob
