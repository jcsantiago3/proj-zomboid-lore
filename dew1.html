WENDY WALKER — DIARY ENTRY 01

ARRIVAL

Bob signed us up into the facility today. Bob said it was part of a cure and recovery program — a place where exposed families are kept safe while research is done on treatment for the infection.

The process was fast and structured. Medical checks, documentation, housing assignment. Everything was handled like a system that already knows exactly what to do with people like us.

They said this was a protected recovery environment. That families here are monitored while medical teams work on stabilization and eventual reintegration.

Johnny seemed calmer almost immediately after arriving. I told myself that was a good sign — that maybe this place really is what they said it is.

- Wendy
