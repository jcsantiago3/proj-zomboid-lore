WENDY WALKER — DIARY ENTRY 02

MISSING

Bob didn’t return from evaluation. Staff keep telling me it’s part of the treatment cycle — that individuals are temporarily separated during advanced assessment stages of the cure program.

But no one can show me where he actually is. There are no records I’m allowed to see, no room assignment, no clear timeline of when evaluation ends or where it happens.

Johnny keeps asking if this is still a cure center. I keep telling him yes, because I don’t know what else to say without making this worse.

- Wendy
