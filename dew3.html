WENDY WALKER — DIARY ENTRY 03

CONTACT

A researcher approached me privately today. "Carl Hardee Jr." is what I saw in his ID. He didn’t behave like the others — he spoke like someone who wasn’t fully aligned with the facility.

He told me Bob is no longer in recovery treatment. He said he is part of controlled experimental research involving infection response and cognitive stabilization... whatever that means.

He gave me a syringe and said it is meant for “stabilization at the point of contact,” when conditions are right.

When I asked what that meant, he only said: "I'll tell you the right time. Wait for my signal."

- Wendy
