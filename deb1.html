BOB WALKER — DIARY ENTRY 01

REGISTRATION

A co-worker from my job told me about this facility — said it was a cure and recovery center for infection exposure cases. Somewhere families could be kept safe while research is being done on treatment and stabilization.

It sounded like the first real structured response I’ve heard of since everything started falling apart outside.

We were registered quickly. Me, Wendy, and Johnny. Basic screening, exposure checks, consent forms for participation in ongoing medical research. Everything felt organized, like they’ve done this hundreds of times already.

They placed us under “protected recovery status.” That’s what they called it.

For the first time in a while, I felt like I made the right decision for my family.

- Bob
